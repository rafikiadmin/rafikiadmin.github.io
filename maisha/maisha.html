Maisha Cottage

We created Maisha Cottage in 2009 to be a place of rest and refreshment for ourselves and others. We hope it is for you, too.

The home is about 15 to 20 minutes drive from Naivasha town. About an hour and a half from Nairobi.

There is a master bedroom loft with a queen size bed, a master bath, and an inside and outside balcony. There are two other bedrooms downstairs that share another bathroom. One room has a queen size bed and the other has a single bed and a bunk bed.

There is a fully equiped kitchen with stove, refridgerator, microwave, and pots and pans and dishes.

There is a dining table for 6+ inside and two more similar size tables outside on the covered porch. There is also a picnic table in the yard, plus many chairs and swings around the home.

There is fireplace inside, a charcoal grill outside, and a fire pit in the yard.
